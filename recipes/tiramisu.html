Tiramisù


Description

Tiramisù is a delicious Italian dessert made with ladyfingers soaked in coffee, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 300 g of ladyfingers
  • 500 g of mascarpone
  • 4 fresh eggs
  • 100 g of sugar
  • 300 ml of cold espresso coffee
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites. Beat the yolks with the sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy. Add the mascarpone and mix well.
  2. Beat the egg whites with a pinch of salt until they form stiff peaks, then gently fold them into the mascarpone mixture.
  3. Quickly dip the ladyfingers into the cold coffee and arrange them in a baking dish to form an even layer. Cover with a layer of mascarpone cream. Repeat the process until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cream.
  4. Dust with unsweetened cocoa powder and let it rest in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours before serving.
