Spaghetti alla carbonara


Description

Carbonara is a classic Roman dish characterized by a creamy sauce made with eggs, guanciale, and pecorino romano.

Ingredients

  • 400 g of spaghetti
  • 150 g of guanciale
  • 4 egg yolks
  • Garlic
  • 100 g of grated pecorino romano
  • Spices
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Cut the guanciale into cubes and fry it in a pan without adding oil until it becomes crispy.
  3. In a bowl, beat the egg yolks with the pecorino and a pinch of black pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the guanciale. Remove the pan from the heat and add the egg and pecorino mixture, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Add the tomatoes with juice and a pinch of sugar.
  6. Plate the spaghetti and add a generous sprinkle of black pepper and pecorino romano to taste.
