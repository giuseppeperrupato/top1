Risotto alla milanese


Description

Risotto alla Milanese is a traditional dish from Milan, famous for its golden yellow color given y saffron and its creamy texture.

Ingredients

  • 320 g of Carnaroli rice
  • 1 liter of meat broth
  • 1 packet of saffron
  • 1 onion
  • 50 g of butter
  • 50 g of grated parmesan
  • 1 glass of dry white wine
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Finely chop the onion and saut√© it in half of the butter until it becomes translucent.
  2. Add the rice to the pan with the onion and toast it for a couple of minutes, stirring continuously.
  3. Pour in the white wine and let the alcohol evaporate.
  4. Add a ladle of hot broth at a time, stirring often and waiting for the liquid to be absorbed before adding more.
  5. Dissolve the saffron in a little hot broth and add it to the risotto halfway through cooking.
  6. When the rice is al dente, turn off the heat and add the remaining butter and parmesan. Stir vigorously to make it creamy.
  7. Let it rest for a minute and then serve the risotto hot.
